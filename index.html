Our Story

Solar812 was founded by 12-year-old Osman Pasha from Hong Kong with a simple mission: to make clean energy accessible to everyone while helping protect our planet.

The name Solar812 represents infinite solar power (8) championed by a 12-year-old innovator. For every Solar812 product sold, we rember that we are contributing to a greener future.

Our products are selected with both performance and sustainability in mind. We believe that technology and ecology can work together to create a better tomorrow.
